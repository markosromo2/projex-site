Scoring Methodology

The Contractor Reliability Index

Three objective inputs. One composite score. No gut feelings. The CRI gives every contractor a number your entire team can stand behind.

Q
Quality Rating
Aggregate review score across all 7 categories
Wq = 33%
Q = (Rating Score / Total Possible) x 100
C
Cost Efficiency
Initial bid vs. final billed — penalizes overruns
Wc = 33%
C = (Initial Bid Cost / Actual Total Cost) x 100
S
Schedule Efficiency
Planned days vs. actual days — penalizes overruns
Ws = 34%
S = (Planned Days / Actual Days) x 100
Final Formula
CRI = Wq(Q) + Wc(C) + Ws(S)
Weights are company-adjustable in your Business Dashboard
Live Example — Apex Civil Contractors
Q (Quality) x 33% 87 x 0.33 = 28.7
C (Cost) x 33% 94 x 0.33 = 31.0
S (Schedule) x 34% 81 x 0.34 = 27.5
CRI Score 87.2
Business accounts can customize Wq, Wc, and Ws weights to match your firm's priorities — e.g., set Wc = 50% if budget overruns are your #1 risk.
